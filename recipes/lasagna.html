Chicken Taco Lasagna

A chicken taco twist on classical lasagna

A little twist to the traditional italian lasagna. Adding the tex-mex flavors gives a completely new view on this classic

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 packets taco seasoning
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered
  2. Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 minutes
  3. Mix 1/2 cup mozzarella, sour creamm, and salsa in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken on top. cover with the remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  5. bake in the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour
